== ESPD-EDM v4.1.0

* The new release v4.1.0 was presented as a central part of the meeting. The list of the model changes was reviewed with details:
** SUBCRITERION simplification is similar to LEGISLATION in ESPD-EDM.
** For the Economic Operator the National Legislation text will be displayed.
** Links to evidence models – if available in eCertis.
* As an example, was presented the UI Mockup with EG Participation in a criminal organisation in Slovenian National Legislation and EU legislation extracted from eCertis.
* Part IV Selection Criteria (C: Technical and professional ability) the relied upon entities was added in: “For works contracts: performance of works of the specified type”, “For supply contracts: performance of deliveries of the specified type.” and “For service contracts: performance of services of the specified type.”
* Evidence block will be promoted at top level so that the Economic Operator can provide the link to the electronic evidence.

== Business Rules – new approach

* ESPD-EDM team presented the ongoing work on Business Rules.
* Aligning with eForms Business Rules style.
** Identifying common code lists from EU Vocabulary.
** Defining Business Groups and Business Terms.
** Identifying Fields and their UBL mapping.
** Defining Business Rules.
* ESPD-EDM specific rules:
** ESPD Document structure.
** Mapping of Criteria to ESPD Document structure.
* OUC is invited to get involved in Business Rules creation and improvement.

== Open discussion: relied upon entity

* Part IV Selection Criteria and the relied upon entity.
** Should each Selection Criterion contain the part where the relied upon entity can be specified?
* In ESPD-EDM v4.1.0 some Selection Criteria have the block for specifying the information about relied upon entities.
* Future versions of ESPD-EDM (v5.x.x) will systematically contain the block for relied upon entities in Selection Criteria.
* Conclusions:
** “Relied upon” element will be added in all criteria in a future release.
** The 2^nd^ question (yes/no element) to be removed from the block.

== Next steps
